ABOUT US

Outline clinics was started in 1974 as a dispensary later promoted to health centre in 1968 and has grown to a modern referrral hospital after upgrading in 1979.
Our clinic plays various roles in the region(marsabit) by providing large range of services at the hospital and through parterships with the greator community,public health and other organisations.
In addition to health care services provided, the hospital offers internship centre for both medical students, clinical medicine(diploma) and attachment for pharmacists
We have our branches countrywide.
